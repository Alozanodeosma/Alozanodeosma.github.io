MIDNIGHT SNACK

Short and surreal first-person horror game where you wander through an eerie forest using the dials of a microwave.

Project info

  • Category: Videogame
  • Genre: Horror
  • Platform: Steam, Itch.io
  • Project date: 2025
  • The project started as a Game Jam horror game and was transformed into a shipped product during the course of 2025. During the development more than 600 people added the game to the Steam wishlist. The game gathered more the 200 reviews on Steam with very positive reviews
